Thiagarajar College of Engineering (TCE) is located in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India.
It is an autonomous, government-aided institution affiliated with Anna University.
TCE offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various engineering and technology disciplines.

The college comprises departments such as Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Information Technology (IT), Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Mechatronics.
All departments offer undergraduate programs, while the CSE and IT departments also provide postgraduate programs.

TCE is well-equipped with modern laboratories, a Wi-Fi-enabled campus, hostels, and a digital library.
The institution’s mission is to deliver quality technical education, encourage innovation, and uphold strong ethical values.

In the previous academic year, the IT and EEE departments achieved a 100% placement record.
The Civil Engineering program is widely recognized as one of the best at TCE.

Additionally, the college offers MCA and MBA programs exclusively for female students.
To gain admission, candidates must have secured a minimum of 95% in their qualifying examinations.

The minimum annual fee structure is ₹1,00,000 for undergraduate programs and ₹1,50,000 for postgraduate programs.
Transportation facilities are available for both male and female students.

TCE maintains partnerships with several leading multinational companies, including Zoho, Tap Academy, Cognizant, HCL, Bass Enterprises, TCS, and Chella Software.
The college celebrates festivals such as Diwali and Pongal, promoting cultural inclusivity.
Freshers are granted free access to the library as part of the college’s student support initiatives.
